General operations

EnvModifyScrollDigital is used to change the envelope (the shape of the zone without constraints) based on the suction temperature parameter. Not all Copeland Scroll™ Digital Compressor require this management.

The following compressors require this management:

  • Copeland ZRD42KCE-TFD, R407C

  • Copeland ZRD48KCE-TFD, R407C

  • Copeland ZRD61KCE-TFD, R407C

  • Copeland ZRD72KCE-TFD, R407C

  • Copeland ZRD81KCE-TFD, R407C

EnvPwr, which must be linked to EnvModifyScrollDigital, checks that the compressor works in the optimal work zone or adopts corrective actions if the compressor moves outside the zone, so as to protect the compressor.

EnvModifyScrollDigital must always be used with the DefCompEnvScrollDigital function block. For definitions of vertex vectors, polygons and their association, refer to documents on DefCompEnvScrollDigital.

How to change envelope shape based on the compressor

Only for the compressors described above, there are two different envelopes relating to the thermodynamic conditions: when the suction temperature exceeds SuctTempRef_SCRDIG, the envelope becomes more closed, as shown in the following image for the ZRD81KCE-TFD compressor.
