General operations

CompVlvs manages compressor valve actions according to CfgCompVlv from the DefCompVlvs and PhRun from CompStep or CompStepless. It sends the valve status back to CompStepless to calculate the current capacity (PwrRun). VlvDout_Info is the structure that contains all the information for the I/O editor.

3: Increase

4: Standby

5: Decrease
