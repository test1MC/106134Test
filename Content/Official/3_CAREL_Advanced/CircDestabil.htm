General operations

The CircDestabil avoids long periods in stable conditions at part load in a multi-compressor circuit, forcing On the compressor that has remained Off.

This function prevents the migration of oil from the crankcase of the compressors that are Off to the crankcase of the compressor that is On: in this way, each compressor will never be without oil when starting up.

 

In a circuit with several compressors in parallel, the differences in temperature/pressure between the compressors that are On and compressors that are Off may cause slow oil migration to the compressors that are On (mostly present in a circuit with a BLDC compressor). Running in this condition for several hours (10 - 15) empties the crankcase of the compressors that are Off. Starting that compressor in this condition could cause the bearings to seize and compressor breakdown.

 

When the circuit is running at part load, the compressor that is Off for the specified period of time (DT_CompOff_CDST) is forced On.

In case of a circuit with a BLDC compressor (CircCfg_CDST=0), the algorithm starts to check the period of time (DT_CompOff_CDST) only if the speed of the BLDC (CompSpeed_CDST) is greater than a threshold (MinCompSpeedThrsh_CDST). Below this threshold, the working condition of the BLDC is not enough to drain the oil from the crankcase of the other compressor that is Off.

An included additional controls the forcing on of the compressors to avoid simultaneous multiple activations : a time (DT_CompOn_CDST) between two forced starts is always respected (time also considered for forcing on between different circuits). The compressors with alarms are not forced on.

 

It is necessary to use a FB for each circuit. The FBs on different circuits are connected together via the Input/Output pin FrcCompDestabil_CDST/In_CDST, so as to exchange data on forcing requests.

The FB manages a maximum of 4 circuits with 4 compressors per circuit. On circuits with less than 4 compressors, to disable the compressors that are not used simply set them to alarm status ( Comp2Alrm_CDST, Comp3Alrm_CDST, Comp4Alrm_CDST = TRUE).

Examples
Example 1: circuit with BLDC Tandem/Trio or 3 OnOff compressors

The FB receives some information in input regarding the status of the compressors in the circuit:

  • CompSpeed_CDST from Info_PWRP.Info_RotorSpeed_rps of Comp_BLDC
  • Comp1On_CDST, Comp2On_CDST, ... from compressor status
  • Comp1Alrm_CDST, Comp2Alrm_CDST, ... from compressor alarm status

And returns the forcing on command for the compressors:

  • FrcOnComp1_CDST, FrcOnComp2_CDST, ... to force the compressors on in the circuit

 

Example 2: circuit with 2 compressors

The inputs Comp3Alrm_CDST and Comp4Alrm_CDST are set to TRUE to disable compressor 3 and 4. In this way the FB can work on a circuit with 2 compressors.

 

Example 3: connection between circuits

The Input/Output pins of the FBs inthe 4 circuits FrcCompDestabilIn_CDST and FrcCompDestabil_CDST are linked to the same variable. In this way, all the circuits are aligned to the status of the other circuits.
