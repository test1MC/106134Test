General operations

ReverseValve checks whether the conditions are met to move the 4-way valve slider to reverse coolant flow.

The request is satisfied when UnitChHp_RV is TRUE and DeltaP_RV is greater than DeltaPThrsh_RV.

If a blackout occurs ReverseValve checks the compressor status and the reversing valve status to align the solenoid valve status to the 4-way slider

 

To perform blackout management, CompStatus_NVRAM_RV and DoutVlvStatus_NVRAM_RV need to be stored in NVRAM.
