General operations

The BLDC_TandemTrio FB manages a circuit comprising a BLDC compressor + 1 or 2 OnOff compressors. It processes the request coming from the DevicesRotation FB, to activate/deactivate the OnOff compressors at the right conditions, related to the BLDC speed and capacity ratio of the compressors. It also controls the positioning of EEV during the switching on and off transients for fixed speed compressors.

 

The algorithm comprises a unit state (from 0 to 6) in which two states are stable (0 and 4) and correspond to the following two operating conditions:

  • without OnOff compressors activated (state 0);
  • with OnOff compressors activated (state 4).

The other states are the transient to switch the OnOff compressors On or Off:

  • from 1 to 3 to manage activation;
  • from 5 to 6 to manage deactivation.

To ensurea correct sequence, the FB inhibits further calls of steps by DevicesRotation using a specific flag WaitOthrDev_TT, until it has completed the previously required action.

If capacity request goes to 0, the FB starts the shut off sequence.

 

Functions
  • It considers the BLDC working zone conditions (BLDC_EnvZone_TT) to evaluate the possibility to switch the OnOff compressorsOn or Off (ZoneAbleToOn_TT or ZoneAbleToOff_TT).

    • Zone 1: any action is allowed;
    • Zones 2, 3, 4, 9: cannot be turned On;
    • Zones 5, 6, 7, 8: cannot be turned Off.
  • It manages the alarm and the forced Off state (DevAvbl_TT = 10 or 6), switching the affected compressor Off immediately.
  • It manages high gas discharge temperature (BLDC_DscgTempZone_TT = 2). When the BLDC speed is already far from the maximum (less than half), it uses the prevent array PrvCirc_TT (in the DevicesRotation FB) to shutdown an OnOff compressor, for at least the prevent time (PrvMinT_TT). As a consequence, the compression ratio decreases, BLDC speeds up thus increasing the mass flow of refrigerant gas through the compressor. The result is a reduction in the gas discharge temperature.
  • It cooperates with the envelope of Comp_BLDC FB to manage when operation is outside the envelope in zones 2, 3, 4, 9 (BLDC_EnvZone_TT). When the speed goes below the threshold for switching the OnOff compressor Off , it uses the prevent array PrvCirc_TT (in the DevicesRotation FB) to shutdown an OnOff compressor, for at least the prevent time (PrvMinT_TT). The prevent condition is reset as soon as the working point comes back inside the envelope and the BLDC speed goes up to the OnOff compressor switching On threshold (FixOnThrsh_TT).
  • If the compressor is forced off manually (DevAvbl_TT = 13), it skips the activation or deactivation sequence of the unit state.
  • It can force the circuit to maximum capacity (GoToMaxCircPwr_TT). When the capacity request is greater than zero, it is possible to force the circuit (i.e. for Defrost), in this case it skips the activation or deactivation sequence and goes to a specific state (PhRun_TT = 7). The switching On between two OnOff compressors is delayed by 10s.

Unit state

 

Below is the description of the unit state as shown above.

 

RUNNING

PhRun_TT = 0

Normal operation without OnOff compressors, only BLDC, which can be On or Off.

When the BLDC reaches the activation speed (FixOnThrsh_TT) and there is request (DevRot_TT array) for rotation (DevicesRotation) and the conditions are inside the envelope (ZoneAbleToOn_TT), then the sequence moves to the ACTIVATION phase (PhRun_TT = 1).

 

ACTIVATION

PhRun_TT = 1

BLDC speed is forced to minimum (BLDC_GoToMinSpeed_TT). When the speed reaches the average value between On and Off thresholds (FixOnThrsh_TT, FixOffThrsh_TT), the sequence moves to the next phase (PhRun_TT = 2).

 

PhRun_TT = 2

The OnOff compressor turns On, the EEV valve is repositioned and the BLDC continues to slow down for another 15s. After the sequence moves to the next phase (PhRun_TT = 3).

 

PhRun_TT = 3

The EEV valve is controlled to avoid repositioning and the BLDC is again controlled based on the envelope. If the BLDC speed is greater than the Off speed threshold (FixOffThrsh_TT), the sequence movesto the RUNNING phase (PhRun_TT = 4).

 

RUNNING

PhRun_TT = 4

Normal operation with at least one OnOff compressor active.

In case of:

  • An activation request for an OnOff compressor not yet processed and the conditions are inside the envelope (ZoneAbleToOn_TT), the sequence moves to the ACTIVATION phase (PhRun_TT = 1).
  • A deactivation request for an OnOff compressor and the conditions are inside the envelope (ZoneAbleToOff_TT), the sequence moves to the DEACTIVATON phase (PhRun_TT = 5).

 

DEACTIVATION

PhRun_TT = 5

The OnOff compressor turns Off, the EEV valve is repositioned and the BLDC is controlled based on the envelope, after 15s the sequence moves to the next phase (PhRun_TT = 6).

 

PhRun_TT = 6

The EEV valve is controlled to avoid repositioning and the BLDC continues to be controlled.

Then if there is:

  • Another OnOff compressor active, the sequence moves to the RUNNING phase 4 (PhRun_TT = 4).
  • Only the BLDC active, the sequence moves to the RUNNING phase 0 (PhRun_TT = 0).

 

Background to "threshold calculation"

In case of BLDC Tandem Trio configuration the arrays of the DevicesRotation the FB must be configured in the following way (4 devices per circuit).

  BLDC OnOff 1 OnOff 2 - BLDC OnOff 1 OnOff 2 - ... BLDC OnOff 1 OnOff 2 -
Index 0 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 ... 28 29 30 31
Circuit 1 2 ... 8
Device 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 ... 29 30 31 32

 

The DevPwr_TT array contains the relative capacity of the compressors on the unit.

 

For further information, see the DevicesRotation help documentation.

 

Threshold calculation

The following formulas are used to calculate the thresholds of capacity request (PwrReq_TT), to control compressor switching On and Off. All Power[x] values are percentages.

 

BLDC switch On threshold:

O n ⁢   B L D C = B L D C _ O n T h r s h _ T T B L D C _ M a x S p e e d _ T T   • P o w e r [ B L D C ]

 

The following two formulas are necessary to find the On and Off thresholds for the OnOff compressors.

  • Capacity request at FixOnThrsh_TT of the BLDC:

A = F i x O n T h r s h _ T T B L D C _ M a x S p e e d _ T T   • P o w e r [ B L D C ]

  • Capacity request at FixOffThrsh_TT of the BLDC plus the capacity of the OnOff compressor/s:

B = F i x O f f T h r s h _ T T B L D C _ M a x S p e e d _ T T   • P o w e r [ B L D C ] + P o w e r [ O n O f f ]

 

OnOff compressor On threshold is the higher result of the formula reported above (A, B).

Activation OnOff 1 = MAX(A, B)

Activation OnOff 2 = MAX(A, B) + Power[OnOff]

...

Repeat for the number of the OnOff compressors on the unit.

 

OnOff compressor Off threshold is the higher result of the formula reported above (A, B).

Deactivation OnOff 1 = MIN(A, B) + Power[OnOff] + ...

Deactivation OnOff 2 = MIN(A, B)

...

Repeat for the number of the OnOff compressors on the unit.

 

 

The capacity of OnOff compressors in a Trio configuration must be the same (DevPwr_TT[1]=DevPwr_TT[2]).

Before switching an OnOff compressor On, the BLDC speed must reach the FixOnThrsh_TT. While to switch an OnOff compressor Off , the BLDC cannot reach the FixOffThrsh_TT (depending on the ratio between compressor capacity).
