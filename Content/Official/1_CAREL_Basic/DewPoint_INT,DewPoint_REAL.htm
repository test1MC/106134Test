Common operations
DewPoint_INT

For _INT version, TAmb and output are expressed in tenths of degrees Celsius (°C/10).

Humidity is in tenths of degrees Celsius(°C/10).

DewPoint_REAL

For _REAL version, TAmb and output are expressed in degrees (°C).

Humidity is in degrees Celsius (°C).
