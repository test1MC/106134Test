Common operations

Atmospheric pressure is expressed in mbar.

AbsHumidity_INT

Humidity is in tenths (100%=1000).

TAmb is expressed in tenth of degrees (°C/10).

Output value is in tenths of water kg/dry .

AbsHumidity_REAL

Relative humidity (100%=100.0)

TAmb is expressed in celsius degrees (°C).

Output value is in water kg/dry air kg.
