General operations

This block is used to read data from the Energy Meter 3 Second Edition.

All data are grouped by phase, as well as the total of all phases

The function block provides the possibility to reset the energy counters inside the device

 

Communication settings

For communication via serial protocol, download the device templates from KSA

EnergyMeter3SE requires the following template

 

Modbus Master

  1. EnergyMeter3SE_MBM_EnergyMeter3SE.txt

 

The endianess of this device is "Little endian swapped (2, 1, 4, 3)"
