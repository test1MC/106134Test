General operations

cpCOe_2_AddrChangeWizard manages the operations to be done in order to change the address extension of a cpCOe_2.

cpCOe_2_AddrChangeWizard works together with cpCOe_2 and a screen loop that is imported when importing the cpCOe_2 worksheet.

 

An Offline Alarm may occur during the wizard

In order to perform this kind of operation, address 207 on the line where the c.pCOe is connected must be free

 

Communication settings

For communications with the c.pCOe, this block uses the cpCOe_2 block
