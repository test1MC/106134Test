General operations

cpCOe_2_UIMng manages the configuration of the IOs on the c.pCOe via user interface, preventing incorrect configurations being sent to the c.pCOe

cpCOe_2_UIMng works together with cpCOe_2 and a screen loop that is imported when importing the cpCOe_2 worksheet.

 

Communication settings
 

For communications with the c.pCOe, this block uses the cpCOe_2 block
