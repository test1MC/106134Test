General operations

This allows parameters from BLDC to be entered in the DATA_EVDEMB structure.

RfrgVlv is aligned with the BLDC numeration: gas type starts from 0.

Inside, BLDC2EVD_Emb_2 moves the gas type by one unit by adding 1.
