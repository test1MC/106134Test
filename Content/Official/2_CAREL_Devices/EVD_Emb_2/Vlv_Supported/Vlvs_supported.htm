 Sanhua L Series

19 October 2015

The L series valve is described on the Sanhua website as a 4-way valve and not as a single-pole valve. The 2 single-pole valves we have are respectively:

  • Electronic Expansion Valve series DPF-T/S (the larger one) Link

  • Electronic Expansion Valve R Series (the smaller one) Link

Tests conducted with the Sanhua DPF -T/S series valve.

The valve has a travel of 0 - 250 steps, whereas the EVD EMB sets a maximum of 500 steps

The phase order is inverted compared to Carel valves (4 - 3 - 2 - 1 rather than 1 - 2 - 3 - 4)
